About

I am a friendly conscientious Financial Adviser who is experienced in giving holistic financial advice. I put my clients needs at the heart of everything I do helping to give them the peace of mind that their future financial goals are met. I have achieved the qualification designation of Chartered Financial Planner through the Chartered Insurance Society (CII) and Personal Finance Society (PFS).

I have worked in the financial services sector since 2011 and have provided regulated financial advice since 2015. During this time I have helped thousands of clients with financial advice. The range of advice has covered investments, retirement planning, pension management and pension transfers, estate and trust planning, financial protection, share scheme and redundacy advice.

Outside of work I am a father to a daughter and avid fitness and music lover. I have played football at various levels throughout my life but my fondest moment's came playing internationally for Anguilla in the Concacaf competitions.
